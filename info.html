Nazar Ro is a Ukraine-born Kyiv based artist. He studied in Kyiv National I. K. Karpenko-Kary Theatre, Cinema and Television University, Ukraine and in International Auteur Base Camp at Scenekunst Højskolen Snoghøj, Denmark.

He likes making things, painting, thinking, writing, photography, videography and directing, having conversations with others, finding solutions to problems, design fonts, making books, arranging spaces, composing music, designing, among other things.

"I like the idea of being a creator instead of focusing on one specific thing you keep freedom of doing whatever you want to create. Freedom of creation allows me to have zero expectations from the viewer, and you can show up with anything, which also means that a chance to face with judgement is smaller."

I am just a creator try to see where I get :)
